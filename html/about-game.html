ABOUT GAME

Atreus has been captured by RA, the god of this world. Do you have what it takes to free him?

Asgard lies in ruins, and the Nine Realms are shattered. While exploring Tyr's temple, Atreus uncovers a gateway to other pantheons but is captured by the Egyptian sun god, Ra. Now, Kratos must journey to the mysterious sands of Egypt, facing powerful deities and deadly challenges to save his son. The fate of father and son hangs in the balance once more.
